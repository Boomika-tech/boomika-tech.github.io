Who We Are
We from League for Environmental Awareness and Protection (LEAP) ,the student community of NITT , raising awareness about environmental issues and to reducing environmental impact as individuals, a college community, and as citizens of the world.We are the Central Environmental Club, dedicated to improving and promoting environmental quality and sustainability on campus and in the community.
Vision
Our aim is to disseminate the urgent issues prevailing in the environment and in the campus among the students of NIT-Trichy.
Mision
We are towards a new approach to spread awareness and educate the students so that they develop a high level of competence in global environmental issues and to labor themselves in finding suitable solutions at the college level itself.
